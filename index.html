Welcome to the MBTA Project

Throughout our time as Boston students, we have experienced many issues with Bostonâ€™s transportation system. From unreliable predictions on T and bus arrivals to waiting upwards of 30 minutes for the next bus to arrive, Boston students are all too familiar with the uncertainty surrounding public transit. Our visualizations aim to discover the distribution of predicted versus actual arrival times across Boston, find hotspots of inefficiency, and prevent the pain of stranding ourselves. These visualizations will allow students to confidently leave Snell that extra crucial minute later, or even avoid trouble spots entirely, opting for more sure routes.

Using our identified datasets, we aim to map how often the mass dot website accurately predicts the true arrival time of the MBTA Orange Line. Additionally, we will aim to graph how many minutes, on average, the Mass DOT predicted time is off from the true departure time.
